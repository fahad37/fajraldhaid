Efficient Flat Surface Cutting

Expert Floor Sawing in UAE

Floor sawing (also known as slab sawing) is the most efficient method for cutting horizontal concrete surfaces such as floors, bridge decks, and pavement. At Fajr Al Dhaid, we use high-powered walk-behind saws to cut expansion joints, remove damaged pavement sections, and create trenches for utility installation.

Our fleet includes both electric and diesel-powered floor saws, allowing us to work in both indoor environments (emission-free) and large outdoor projects. We ensure speed, precision, and safety in every cut.

Why Floor Sawing?

  • Speed: Floor saws are powerful and can cut through concrete and asphalt quickly.
  • Depth: Capable of cutting up to 600mm deep depending on the equipment used.
  • Versatility: Suitable for reinforced concrete, green concrete, and asphalt.

Applications

We provide floor sawing for highway construction, warehouse floor repair, plumbing trenching, and creating expansion joints to prevent concrete cracking.
