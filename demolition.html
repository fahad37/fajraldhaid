Demolition Works

Safe, Controlled, and Efficient Demolition

Professional Demolition Services

Fajr Al Dhaid offers comprehensive demolition services, ranging from selective interior demolition to total structural removal. We approach every project with a focus on safety, environmental compliance, and efficiency.

Our team is experienced in dismantling concrete structures, removing partitions, and executing controlled demolition in sensitive environments. We use a combination of robotic demolition equipment, hydraulic crushers, and traditional methods to get the job done right.

Our Demolition Capabilities

  • Selective Demolition: Removing specific parts of a building while preserving the rest (e.g., walls, slabs, staircases).
  • Interior Strip-out: Removing all non-structural elements for renovation projects.
  • Concrete Breaking: Breaking up concrete foundations, slabs, and columns.
  • Debris Removal: We ensure all demolition waste is collected and disposed of responsibly.

Safety First

Demolition is a high-risk activity. We adhere to strict safety protocols and use advanced equipment to minimize risks to our workers and your property.
