Track-Mounted Precision Cutting

Precision Wall Sawing Solutions

Wall sawing employs a circular blade on a track-mounted machine. This track system allows the saw to climb walls or cut along ceilings with extreme accuracy. Fajr Al Dhaid offers professional wall sawing services for creating precise openings in vertical or steep surfaces.

Our wall sawing equipment is remotely operated for safety and can cut through heavily reinforced concrete, brick, and masonry. It is the method of choice when you need clean, straight cuts for doorways, windows, or ventilation units in existing structures.

Key Advantages

  • Accuracy: The track system ensures perfectly straight cuts every time.
  • Versatility: Can be used on vertical walls, steep inclines, and even inverted for ceiling cuts.
  • Depth: Capable of cutting through thick concrete walls up to significant depths.
  • Safety: Remote operation keeps operators at a safe distance from the cutting zone.

Common Uses

Wall sawing is commonly used for creating openings for staircases and elevators, cutting openings for large doors and windows, and separation cuts for demolition projects.
