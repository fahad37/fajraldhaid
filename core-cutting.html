Expert Core Cutting in UAE

Fajr Al Dhaid provides top-tier core cutting services across Sharjah, Dubai, and the entire UAE. Core cutting (or core drilling) is the process of drilling perfectly round holes through concrete walls, floors, and other structures. This technique is essential for creating openings for plumbing pipes, electrical conduits, HVAC ducts, and telecommunication lines.

Our team uses advanced diamond-tipped core drill bits, which allow us to cut through reinforced concrete, brick, block, and stone with extreme precision and minimal noise or vibration. This ensures the structural integrity of your building remains intact while achieving the exact specifications required for your project.

Why Choose Our Core Cutting Service?

  • Precision & Accuracy: We can drill holes of various diameters and depths with millimeter-level accuracy.
  • Speed & Efficiency: Our modern equipment allows for rapid execution, keeping your project on schedule.
  • Minimal Disruption: Low noise and vibration levels make our services suitable for occupied buildings.
  • Cleanliness: We use wet drilling techniques to minimize dust and ensure a clean work environment.

Applications

Our core cutting services are ideal for:

  • Plumbing and drainage pipes
  • Electrical cables and conduits
  • Air conditioning vents and ducts
  • Fire protection systems
  • Anchor bolt holes
  • Testing concrete strength (sample extraction)
