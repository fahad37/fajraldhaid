Heavy-Duty Chemical Anchoring

Fajr Al Dhaid provides specialized chemical anchoring services for structural applications where high load-bearing capacity is required. Chemical anchoring involves using a high-strength resin to bond steel studs, bolts, or rebar into concrete, brick, or stone.

This method is superior to mechanical anchors in many situations as it does not create expansion stress in the substrate, allowing for installation close to edges and closer spacing between anchors.

Applications

  • Rebar Doweling: Extending existing concrete structures by bonding new rebar into old concrete.
  • Steel Structure Fixing: Anchoring steel beams, columns, and base plates.
  • Machinery Installation: Securing heavy industrial machinery to concrete floors.
  • Crash Barriers & Railings: Fixing safety barriers and handrails.

Our Process

We ensure the hole is drilled to the correct diameter and depth, thoroughly cleaned (critical for bond strength), and then injected with high-quality chemical resin before inserting the anchor. We follow strict manufacturer guidelines to guarantee the load capacity.
