Concrete Cutting Services

Advanced Sawing Solutions for Any Structure

Professional Concrete Cutting

Fajr Al Dhaid specializes in high-precision concrete cutting for residential, commercial, and industrial projects across the UAE. Our concrete cutting services utilize state-of-the-art diamond blade technology to slice through reinforced concrete with ease and accuracy.

Whether you need to create openings for doors, windows, elevators, or staircases, or require modifications to existing structures, our skilled technicians ensure clean, straight cuts every time. We understand the complexities of construction sites and prioritize safety and efficiency in all our operations.

Our Capabilities

  • Slab Sawing: Ideal for cutting horizontal flat surfaces such as floors, bridge decks, and pavement.
  • Wall Sawing: Perfect for creating precise openings in vertical walls for HVAC, doors, and windows.
  • Wire Sawing: Used for large, thick, or complex concrete structures that traditional saws cannot handle.

Benefits of Our Service

Choosing Fajr Al Dhaid means opting for quality. Our methods produce no structural damage, no dust hazards (wet cutting), and require no patching up after the job is done. We leave a smooth finish ready for the next stage of construction.
